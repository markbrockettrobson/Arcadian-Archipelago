# The Crew of the Pearly Wave

The Pearly Wave is a Horizon Line Cooperative vessel — worker-owned, crew-run, and contracted to carry passengers and supplies as part of the expedition flotilla. Every person aboard holds shares in the ship. Captain Maelis Thorn was elected to her role by the crew and serves at their confidence. Profits from the contract are distributed among everyone when they return.

This is not how most ships work, and the crew know it. They are proud of it.

To passengers used to faction vessels or commercial shipping, the atmosphere takes some getting used to. Crew members speak their minds, push back on decisions they think are wrong, and hold a shipboard assembly every few days to discuss operations. It is noisier than a conventional ship. It is also, by most measures, well run.

They have been living alongside you for weeks at sea. By now, most of them are familiar faces.
