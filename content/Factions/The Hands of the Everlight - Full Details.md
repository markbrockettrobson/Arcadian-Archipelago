### **Logistics & Hosting**

Despite being the emotional and moral catalyst for the expedition, the Hands of the Everlight possess no ships of their own. Instead, their healers and supplies are distributed across the vessels of the various international factions a situation that has birthed a frustrating "Competitive Hospitality." These host nations view the presence of Sarenrae's faithful as a badge of moral legitimacy, using the healers as living proof of their "charitable" intent to justify their own territorial or commercial greed. While these factions vie for the prestige of hosting High Healer Valerius, offering her the finest cabins and performative deference, they consistently ignore her demands for speed. To the flotilla's leadership, the healers are a symbolic shield against public scrutiny; to the healers, they are prisoners of a "bloated anchor" that prioritizes comfortable bureaucracy over the lives they were sent to save.

### **Allies**

While the Hands of the Everlight's frustration extends to nearly every faction in the flotilla, they have found common cause with [The Gray Corsairs](The%20Gray%20Corsairs.md) on matters of humanitarian principle. Both factions prioritize the welfare of the archipelago's people over political maneuvering, and both view colonial exploitation with deep suspicion.

The alliance is not without friction the Hands' religious pacifism doesn't always align with the Gray Corsairs' willingness to use force against slavers and tyrants. High Healer Valerius has expressed discomfort with what she calls the Corsairs' "righteous violence," even when directed at clear injustice. However, both groups recognize they share more in common with each other than with factions that view the archipelago primarily as commercial or political opportunity.

### Notable NPCs

* **[High Healer Valerius](../NPCs/Flotilla/Hands%20of%20the%20Everlight/High%20Healer%20Valerius.md):** The faction's fiery spokesperson, known for her refusal to use "polite" diplomatic language during council meetings. A radical pacifist making up for her lack in martial violence with verbal fury, she has a temper to fear and a lot to be angry about.

* **[Adjudicator Kaelen](../NPCs/Flotilla/Hands%20of%20the%20Everlight/Adjudicator%20Kaelen.md)**: The person who walks into a room five minutes after Valerius has stormed out, offering apologies and smoothing over "misunderstandings." He is far more pragmatic, understanding that for all the faults of the flotilla, they need ships and supplies they would otherwise just not have.

* **[Champion Elara](../NPCs/Flotilla/Hands%20of%20the%20Everlight/Champion%20Elara.md)**: Champion of Sarenrae, tasked with the impossible job of protecting a pacifist leader who refuses to stay out of danger. She often agrees with Valerius's anger but respects Kaelen's methods, acting as the bridge between the two.
